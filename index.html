About

With nearly 20 years of technical writing experience across diverse industries (from ITSM to IoT) and an academic background in Journalism and Anthropology, I love helping people understand how things work in complex and technically challenging environments, so that they can do their jobs skillfully.

I can set up, manage, create and maintain user documentation for hardware and software products. I have mentored others and have extensive training experience.

My core skills are:

  • Excellent, clear and concise written communication.
  • Meticulous approach to proofreading and editing.
  • Quality-focused, adhering to ISO and internal standards.
  • Friendly and approachable, building good relationships with people throughout the organisation.

Services

What writing services do you need?

Technical writing consultant

Let's collaborate to create documentation that works for you. I can help you understand and plan what you need for clear, effective documentation.

Guides / manuals / online help

Do you need user, developer, or quick start guides for your products and services? I write clear, comprehensive guides for both online and print format, using single-sourced content. I can also provide helpful graphics.
